About Me

My name is Theodore R Freeman, but I go by Ted.
I'm a complete nerd, artist, pilot, motorcyclist, handyman and, outdoorsman.

I am currently living in Cedar City, UT and pursuing a career in cybersecurity. Most recently, I passed the SY0-601 exam for the CompTIA Security + certificate. I'm really excited to get going in this field and my goal is to eventually move into penetration testing and bug bounty as I build on my understanding of computer systems and how everything works together!

I started Highschool in Longmont Colorado, where I grew up, but I was given an amazing opportunity to finish my last year and a half in South Korea at Korea International School. I learned a lot at KIS that has helped me change my perspective, think about the bigger picture, and be open to new experiences.

After graduating, I went back to Colorado to attend college at CU Boulder. There I studied Computer Science, but also had amazing classes in philosophy, mythology, art and art history. One of the classes I found most interesting was intro to cybersecurity taught by Professor Daniel Massey. It was my first exposure to a lot of the concepts of cyber security and was really intriguing. That first class is what brought me onto this career path. I earned my BA in Computer Science and minor in Nordic Studies in May of 2018.

After completing my degree, I shifted my focus to aviation. I come from a family of pilots, my father being one of the best aerobatic pilots in the country. He took on the role of my instructor, and six months later I earned my private pilot certificate, tailwheel and high-performance endorsements. I intend to one day continue my flight training and earn at least my Instrument and Multi-engine Ratings.
